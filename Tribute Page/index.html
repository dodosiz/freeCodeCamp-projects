Pyrros Dimas
The golden weightlifter

Pyrros Dimas (born on 13 October 1971) is a retired Greek weightlifter. He is currently involved in politics, as a member of the Greek parliament, representing the Panhellenic Socialist Movement. Dimas is widely considered as one of the greatest weightlifters of all time, having been three times Olympic Gold Medalist (in his fourth Olympiad, a knee injury notwithstanding, he won the Bronze medal), and three times World Champion.

Olympic Games

  • Gold 1992 Barcelona
  • Gold 1996 Atlanta
  • Gold 2000 Sydney
  • Bronze 2004 Athens

World Championships

  • Gold 1993 Melbourne
  • Gold 1995 Guangzhou
  • Gold 1998 Lahti
  • Silver 1999 Athens

European Championships

  • Gold 1995 Warszawa
  • Silver 1998 Riesa
  • Bronze 1992 Szekszard
  • Bronze 1993 Sofia
